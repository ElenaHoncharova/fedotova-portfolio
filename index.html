My Story

Hi, I'm Volodymyra Fedotova, a 3D motion designer. Attention to detail, quality of work, and being a quick learner, are some of my distinctive professional values. I am at a professional stage where I am able to create a full-fledged 3D video on my own, which includes direction, animation, look development, and post-production.

Before diving into motion design, I spent three years as a video editor. That helped me to develop visual storytelling skills and a very good understanding of video production principles.
